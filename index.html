How did this project come about?

Hi, my name is Kate. In While I was surfing on Instagram, I came across a candle and my sense of beauty urgently informed me that we needed such a candle. But I didn’t have the opportunity to order.

I couldn’t forget about the candle, but the idea came to my mind: “Maybe I can make it myself?” The planting was quite impulsive. The preparation process for making a candle was long but interesting.
